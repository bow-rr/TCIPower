Introduction

Tcipower was founded in 1996 and mainly engaged in the research, development, production and sales of complete electric power equipment and its accessories. 'TCIPower' has become a modern enterprise group integrating intelligent R & D, intelligent manufacturing, intelligent technology, intelligent service, clean energy and capital operation. The company has the test report and qualification certificate of series products, and the qualification conditions for EPC (one-stop) business undertaking large-scale power projects. 'TCIPower'specializes in power system and automation research, high voltage and insulation technology research, power electronics and power transmission research, electrical theory and new technology research.
